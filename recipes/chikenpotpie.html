Chicken Pot Pie

Description

Chicken Pot Pie is a pie filled with chicken, gravy,
corn, carrots, and peas.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Vegetables
  • Butter and Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Broth and Milk
  • Pie Crusts

Steps

  1. Make the fillings
  2. Fill and bake the pie
  3. Cool and store properly
